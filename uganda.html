About Uganda

Uganda is a landlocked country in East Africa. Known as the "Pearl of Africa," it is renowned for its lush landscapes, diverse wildlife, and vibrant culture. Uganda is home to the source of the Nile River, mountain gorillas in Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, and the famous Rwenzori Mountains.

The country boasts 10 national parks and numerous wildlife reserves, making it a premier destination for safari adventures and nature enthusiasts.

Top Attractions

Murchison Falls

Witness the mighty Nile forced through a narrow gorge creating one of Africa's most powerful waterfalls.

Mountain Gorillas

Come face-to-face with endangered mountain gorillas in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest.

Source of the Nile

Visit the legendary source of the Nile River in Jinja, a historic and scenic location.

Queen Elizabeth National Park

Explore this diverse park famous for its tree-climbing lions and hippos.
