Website Planning Document

Uganda Tourism - The Pearl of Africa

1. Site Name

Uganda Tourism - The Pearl of Africa

Reason: This name was chosen because Uganda is famously known as "The Pearl of Africa" due to its stunning natural beauty, diverse wildlife, and warm hospitality. The name immediately communicates the site's purpose while leveraging the country's well-established nickname to create instant recognition and appeal.

2. Site Purpose

The Uganda Tourism website will provide comprehensive information and services to help travelers plan their visit to Uganda, including:

  • Detailed information about Uganda's national parks, wildlife, and natural attractions
  • Tour packages and safari booking services
  • Cultural experiences and community tourism opportunities
  • Travel guides, visa information, and practical travel tips
  • Accommodation recommendations and booking assistance
  • Photo galleries and virtual tours of popular destinations

3. Scenarios

Scenario 1: A family from the United States is planning their first trip to Africa and wants to see mountain gorillas in their natural habitat. They need to know the best time to visit, what permits are required, how much it will cost, and what other activities they can combine with their gorilla trekking experience.

Scenario 2: A solo backpacker traveling through East Africa has two weeks to explore Uganda. They want to know which national parks offer the best wildlife viewing on a budget, how to get around using public transportation, and what safety precautions they should take while traveling alone.

Scenario 3: A couple celebrating their anniversary wants to experience both adventure and relaxation. They're interested in white-water rafting on the Nile River, visiting Murchison Falls, and ending their trip with a luxury safari experience. They need help planning an itinerary that balances excitement with comfort.

4. Color Scheme

The following colors have been selected to reflect Uganda's natural beauty and national identity:

#2E5A88
Primary
#F4A460
Secondary
#228B22
Accent

Color Usage:

  • #2E5A88 (Deep Blue - Primary): Used for headers, navigation bars, footer sections, and primary headings. Represents the clear blue skies and waters of Uganda.
  • #F4A460 (Sandy Brown - Secondary): Used for secondary headings, borders, highlights, and call-to-action buttons. Represents the warm African earth and savanna landscapes.
  • #228B22 (Forest Green - Accent): Used for accent borders, icons, and interactive elements. Represents Uganda's lush tropical forests and vegetation.
  • #333333 (Dark Gray): Used for body text to ensure readability.
  • #FFFFFF (White): Used for backgrounds to provide clean, readable content areas.
  • #F5F5F5 (Light Gray): Used for section backgrounds to create visual separation.

5. Typography

The following fonts have been selected for optimal readability and visual appeal:

Primary Font: Open Sans

  • Font Family: 'Open Sans', Arial, sans-serif
  • Application: Body text, paragraphs, lists, and general content
  • Reason: Open Sans is a highly legible, humanist sans-serif typeface that works well for both screen and print. Its clean, friendly appearance makes it perfect for tourism content that needs to be accessible and easy to read.

Secondary Font: Montserrat

  • Font Family: 'Montserrat', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif
  • Application: Headings (h1-h6), navigation elements, and titles
  • Reason: Montserrat is a geometric sans-serif typeface with a modern, professional appearance. Its bold, distinctive character makes it ideal for creating visual hierarchy and drawing attention to important information.
