About Uganda

Region

Uganda is a landlocked country in East Africa, known for its remarkable biodiversity, including mountain gorillas in Bwindi Impenetrable National Park and diverse wildlife in Queen Elizabeth National Park. The source of the Nile River lies within its borders, and the country is home to Africa's largest lake, Lake Victoria.
